About us

We are an indie game developer.

Developing a new game

Deathboat! is a Survival Horror and Puzzle game. we will be the only survivor of the attack of a monster on the high seas. Our goal will be to escape from the ship alive, solving the various puzzles that we find after the disaster caused by the monster, while avoiding the monster.

Our Logo

Improving our skills

We are a group of students from the Rey Juan Carlos University. Our goal with this project is to enter in the world of video game development.

Enemy Turn Around

This is a concept art of the enemy.

We can visualize a concept art with the turn around of the character, it was created using the Krita application. We present the black devil, the antagonist of the story, he will be the enemy that we will have to avoid while we try to solve the puzzles and escape from the cruise ship.

  • Artist: Ivan Gomez Ortega

Game Cover

Here is an idea for a game cover

This is a design of how the cover of our game would be, we can see the black devil the antagonist as the main element of the cover. It was designed using Krita. The game is currently thought to be only playable online, the cover of the game is part of the development of concept arts!

  • Artist: Ivan Gomez Ortega
